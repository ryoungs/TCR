The Objective of The Cat Room Website is to experiment with different presentation of the Cats at the County Shelter and give viewers more direct access to pictures and information about the Cats in the Shelter as well as Cats in Foster Care.


All of the pictures and data comes from the Anne Arundel County Animal Care and Control primary webpages. Using The Cat Room web page is intended to be a bit more accessable and easer to reach. In addition, we hope to broaden the exposure with links to "The Cat Room" in social media. So expect changes and an ocasional oops. We hope it is useful and perhaps gets an occasional adoption
