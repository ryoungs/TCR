About

Come Visit the Adoptable Cats at Anne Arundel County Animal Care and Control

411 Maxwell Frye Road, Millersville, MD. 21108

The Objective of The Cat Room Website is to present the Cats at the County Shelter in a slightly different light and give viewers more direct access to pictures and information about Cats in the Shelter as well as Cats in Foster Care. For more photos and information about fosters, click here and got to bottom of the page!

The Cat Room follows the terms and conditions of the Anne Arundel County Animal Care and Control (AACACC). All of the pictures and data come from the AACACC primary webpages but with just one click. Using The Cat Room web page is intended to be more accessable and easer to reach. In future, look for links to "The Cat Room" in social media. So expect changes and an ocasional oops. We hope it is useful and perhaps results in an occasional adoption

Finally, The Cat Room will be a place where historical and day to day statistics and references are presented.
