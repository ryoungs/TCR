About

Come Visit the Adoptable Cats at Anne Arundel County Animal Care and Control

411 Maxwell Frye Road, Millersville, MD 21108

The objective of "The Cat Room" is to present Cats at Animal Care and Control in a slightly different light. We hope to give potental adoptors and those looking for lost animals more direct access to pictures and information. For more photos and information about fosters, click here and go to bottom of the foster page!

The Cat Room follows the terms and conditions of Anne Arundel County Animal Care and Control (AACACC). All the photos and data come from the primary AACACC webpages; with just one click access. The Cat Room is intended to be more accessible and quicker to reach; it is especially helpful for those looking for a lost cat as it combines information (age, length of stay, sex, etc.) with a photo. In future, look for links to "The Cat Room" in social media. So expect changes and an ocasional oops. We hope it is useful and results in more adoptions and redemptions.

Finally, The Cat Room will be a place where historical and day to day statistics and references are presented.
